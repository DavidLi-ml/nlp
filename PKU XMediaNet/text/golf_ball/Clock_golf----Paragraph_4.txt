As of 2016 Jaques still sell equipment for the game, comprising a set of 12 markers numbered I to XII (with spikes on the back for insertion into the lawn), balls, and a pre-formed hole with flag, with or without a putter. Metal roman numbers have also been made by F.H.Ayres of London and Hamleys. The firm of A.G. Spalding of New York manufactured a set using numbers 1-12 around 1900, and in the late 1920s the firm of P.S.P. Inc sold a stylish set of 1-12, painted alternately red and white, presented in a tin under the name "Round the Clock Golf". A Danish firm offers to install a prefabricated clock golf playing area with artificial grass surrounded by a granite border.
