In the United Kingdom, a Shotgun Certificate (SGC) is required to possess a "Section 2" shotgun. The round number restrictions apply only to the magazine, not the chamber, so it is legal to have a single-barreled semi-auto or pump-action shotgun that holds three rounds in total, or a shotgun with separate chambers (which would need to also be multi-barrelled).
