Sinpo-class submarine are North Korea's newest submarine class. Prior to 2015, there were only satellite images of these submarines. On May 9th 2015, the first Sinpo-class launched the first KN-11 ballistic missile under the supervision of Kim Jong Un himself. The test was successful. Since then, there have been five test firings of the KN-11, which can be nuclear armed. It is reported that North Korea is building five more Sinpo-class submarines.
