In 1910 Britain officially adopted the .303 British Mark VII cartridge variant loaded with a 11.3 grams (174 gr) flat base spitzer bullet that had a ballistic coefficient (G1 BC) of approximately 0.467. The .303 British Mark VII cartridge had a muzzle velocity of 744 m/s (2,441 ft/s) and a maximum range of approximately 2,743 m (3,000 yd).
