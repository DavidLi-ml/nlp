In the US and less-so in Canada, nearly all pickups are sold with automatic transmissions. The Cummins diesel-equipped Ram is the only full-size truck available with a clutch. It has an ultra-low first-gear ratio for heavy hauling. While the Chevrolet Colorado, Nissan Frontier, and Toyota Tacoma charge for automatic transmission, Fords come with an Automatic transmission at no extra cost in North America.
