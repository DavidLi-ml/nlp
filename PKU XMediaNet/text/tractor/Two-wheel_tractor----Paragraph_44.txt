Linshu Mingsin Machinery Co., Ltd., located in Linshu County, Shandong Province, is the special manufacturing factory for the walking tractors, power tiller and small farm tractors. This factory could manufacture the power tillers from 2 hp to 10 hp, the walking tractors from 6 hp to 22 hp. To cover the different kinds of field and plants, this factory designed the different models walking tractor and make the walking tractor to meet the field like paddy field or dry field, and also the different kinds of plants and using habit of the farmers. The website of this factory is http://www.mingsin.com.
