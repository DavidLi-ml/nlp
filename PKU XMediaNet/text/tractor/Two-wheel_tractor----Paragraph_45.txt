Located in Kerala in southern India, KAMCO in cooperation with Kubota, Japan, startes manufacturing in 1973, manufacturers 3, 4.5, 9 & 12 hp (8.9 kW) two-wheel tractor with a range of attachments, a two-wheel tractor walk behind 3.5 hp 120 centimeter reaper and a small 5 hp (3.7 kW) [2]. It currently sells nearly 12000 of its 2-wheel tractors per year.
