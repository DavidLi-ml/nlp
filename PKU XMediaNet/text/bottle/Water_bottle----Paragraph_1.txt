A water bottle is usually made of plastic, glass, sheepskin or metal. Water bottles are available in different shapes, colors and sizes. In the past, water bottles were sometimes made of wood, bark or leather. Water bottles can be either disposable or reusable. Reusable water bottles can also be used for liquids such as juice, iced tea, alcoholic beverages or soft drinks. This water bottle form can help save the environment, through reducing waste.
