Blade length was usually from 69 to 81 centimetres (27 to 32 in); however, examples exist from 58 to 100 centimetres (23 to 39 in). Pommels were most commonly of the 'Brazil-nut' type from around 1000-1200 AD, with the 'wheel' pommel appearing in the 11th and predominating from the 13th to 15th centuries.
