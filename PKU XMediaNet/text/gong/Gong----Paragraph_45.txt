In Java, Gong making is a religious ritual. Seven men are needed, they take mythological names and undergo rites of purification. A special bronze alloy called gongon is needed and details are at above. However today, they prefer to melt down old gamelan instruments.. Hot metal is poured and 12-18 inches diameter, 2-3 inches thick puck been made. The men works in under darkened hut to see where to hit heated metal by its color. Hammer strikes makes the metal expanded to outside and when this is finished, the metal plate hung and if it does not resonates, it is melt down again. Gongs are national treasure of Java and goes to 7 feet diameter. The disadvantage of Javanese gongs, that they are prone to shutter with additional hammering.
