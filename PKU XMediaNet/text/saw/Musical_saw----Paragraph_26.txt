Mr. Liming Chen was born in a family deeply rooted in musical tradition in China. He learned cello at an early age and learned how to play the musical saw from his father. He became one of the most outstanding musical saw performers in the world and received the "0-1" award from the American Guild of Musical Artists. In addition, he was also the winner of the "Outstanding Talent" Award from the U.S.A. Association of Musicians. His first graduate students won the first four prizes in the teen division of the Musical Saw Contest in California, held in July 2011. Mr. Liming Chen is currently a Board Member of the Shanghai Association Musician's in from China and continues his legacy in the city of New York.
