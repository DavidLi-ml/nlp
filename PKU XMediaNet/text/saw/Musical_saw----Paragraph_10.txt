David Weiss, also known for his virtuoso performances on the Oboe, English Horn, and Piano. Weiss was the first chair, for the Oboe, with the Los Angeles Philharmonic, Music Professor at USC, Instructor, and alumni with the Music Academy of the West in Santa Barbara, California, and Board Member of the Young Musicians Foundation. Weiss had won multiple times, the Musical Saw competition held on Ojai, California, and then went on to judge the up-and-coming musicians in later years. Weiss was featured on the Tonight Show with Johnny Carson, centered around the release of his new album titled; Virtuoso Saw. Not his first musical release, Weiss is also known for his break out performances on the Weiss Family Woodwinds albums.
