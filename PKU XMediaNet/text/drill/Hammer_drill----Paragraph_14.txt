German company Fein patented a "Bohrmaschine mit elektro-pneumatischem Schlagwerk" in 1914. German company Bosch produced the first "Bosch-Hammer" around 1932 in mass production. The US-american company Milwaukee Electric Tool Corporation states that in 1935, it was selling a lightweight 1/4-inch electric hammer drill (cam-action).
