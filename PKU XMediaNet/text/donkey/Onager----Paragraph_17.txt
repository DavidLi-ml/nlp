The onagers' favored habitats consist of desert plains, semi-deserts, oases, arid grasslands, savannahs, shrublands, steppes, mountainous steppes and mountain ranges. The Turkmenian kulan and Mongolian wild asses are known to live in hot and colder deserts. It is estimated by the IUCN that there are about 2,8000 mature individuals in total in the wild.
