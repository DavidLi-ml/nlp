In Japanese, the word "klaxon" (クラクション, kurakushon) refers to car horns in general, and in verb form (kurakushon suru (クラクションする)) refers to sounding the horn. This is also true in languages such as French (French: klaxon), Italian (Italian: clacson), Dutch (Dutch: claxon), Russian (Russian: клаксон), Polish (Polish: klakson), Spanish (Spanish: claxon), Romanian (Romanian: claxon), Czech (Czech: klakson), Turkish (Turkish: klakson) and Indonesian (Indonesian: klakson).
