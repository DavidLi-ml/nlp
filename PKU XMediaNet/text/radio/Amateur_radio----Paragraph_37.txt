In the UK secondary "letters" are used to identify the Stations location - thus a callsign G7OOE becomes GM7OOE when that operator is operating a station in Scotland. "GM" - Scotland, G(E) - England - however the (E) may be omitted, "GW" - Wales, more information from the Ofcom (the UK Radio & Media Licensing Authority) website - www.ofcom.gov.uk
